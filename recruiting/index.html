Why Rugby?

More Than Just a Sport

Rugby builds character, camaraderie, and champions.

Brotherhood

Rugby is built on teamwork and trust. You'll forge lifelong friendships with teammates who push you to be your best on and off the field.

No Experience Needed

Many of our best players had never played rugby before joining. We welcome athletes from all backgrounds and teach you everything from the ground up.

Compete Against Other Schools

We compete in the Lonestar Rugby Conference at Divsion One level

Full-Body Fitness

Rugby is one of the most demanding sports in the world. You'll be in the best shape of your life while having a blast doing it.

Represent UTSA

Wear the blue and orange with pride. Being part of UTSA Rugby means representing your university and your city on the field.

Year-Round Activity

From fall practices to spring competitions and summer training camps, there's always something going on with UTSA Rugby.
